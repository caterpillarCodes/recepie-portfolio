Ghormeh sabzi (Iran)

Ghormeh sabzi or Khoresht sabzi, also spelled qormeh sabzi, is an Iranian herb stew. It is considered the national dish and is a very popular dish in Iran.

Paella (Spain)

Paella is a classic Spanish rice and served in one pan. Although paella originates from Valencia, it’s recognized as the national food of Spain and there are several different varieties.

Salmon Poke Bowl (Hawaii)

A classic Hawaiian preparation, poke is a salad of cubed raw fish—traditionally ahi (yellowfin tuna) but also made with salmon, tofu, or even watermelon—marinated in sesame oil and soy sauce.
